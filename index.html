Frequently Asked Questions

Above you will find our most frequently asked questions. Should you have any further questions please contact your representative.

How do you select the businesses you call?
Most businesses that are called by NAPRO are selected by a small local survey where the community is asked whom they recommend for different services. Additionally, we call other businesses from within your local phonebook.
How was my business recommended?
A small local survey was taken in your local community asking what businesses would be recommended for certain services. The details on whom performs the survey and how it is conducted varies by location.
What length of time will my advertisement run?
Length of time an advertisement will run varies by the product type. Most of our products run from 6 months to 4 years. Pharmacy Bags are targeted to work for around 1 year.
